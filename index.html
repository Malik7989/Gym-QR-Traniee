⚠️ Important Safety Measures

✅

Consult a doctor if you're new to working out, over 40, or have health conditions

✅

Always warm up for 5-10 minutes before starting your workout

✅

Wear proper gym attire and supportive shoes

✅

Stay hydrated before and during your workout

✅

Learn proper equipment usage and start with lighter weights

✅

Use a spotter for heavy lifts and check your surroundings

✅

Stop immediately if you feel dizzy, nauseous, or experience chest pain

How to Use Our QR Scanner

1

Access the Scanner

Click on the "Scanner" link in the navigation menu to access the QR code scanner.

2

Allow Camera Access

When prompted, allow camera access to enable the QR code scanning functionality.

3

Scan the QR Code

Point your camera at the QR code on any gym equipment. Hold steady until the code is recognized.

4

View Instructions

Once scanned, you'll see detailed workout instructions and video demonstrations for the equipment.

5

Alternative: Upload Image

If scanning isn't possible, you can upload a photo of the QR code using the upload button.
